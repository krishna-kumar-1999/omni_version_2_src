Microsoft SSO Settings

Create Azure APP

  • Login to your Microsoft 365 account and go to "Azure Active Directory" -> "Enterprise Application"
  • Click on 'Create own Application'.
  • Then it will ask for your 'App Name'.
  • Then Check on the third option and submit to create App.

Configure Single Sign on

  • Previous steps will get you to your Azure App.
  • Then you need to Click on 'Set up Single Sign on'.
  • Next Click on 'SAML'.
  • Then Edit you Basic SAML configuration.

Basic SAML configuration

  • Click 'EDIT' on Basic SAML configuration Block.
  • Enter your Entity ID manually.
  • Next Enter your Reply URL(Assertion Consumer Service URL) as ' {{SsoReplyURl}} '
  • Then Click 'Save'

Azure AD Identifier

  • Assuming your in the Setup Sigle Sign on with SAML Pgae in your Azure APP.
  • Go to the 4th Step 'Set Up - APP'.

Add Users to Azure APP

  • Go to your Azure App dashboard.
  • Click on the 'user Groups'.
  • Next click the 'Add users' button.
  • Select the users that you want and click update.
